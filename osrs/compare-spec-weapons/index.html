The Math Behind the Calculator

Whenever we use our special attack weapon, we gain damage by speccing with it, but we also lose damage by not attacking with our main weapon.
To account for this, we calculate how much extra damage the special attack does compared to your main weapon:

Extra Damage = (Spec DPS − Main DPS) × Spec Attack Speed

It is important that we use DPS (damage per second) instead of average hit because the attack speed of your main weapon and special attack weapon need not be the same.
For example, if our main weapon is a scythe (5t), then a zaryte crossbow spec would lose out on 5 ticks of scythe dps, but a voidwaker spec would only lose out on 4 ticks of scythe dps.

The last factor to account for is how much special attack energy your special attack weapons use.
For example, if we are comparing voidwaker (50%) against zaryte crossbow (75%), we get ×1.5 as many voidwakers as zcb specs. One way to account for this is to multiply the extra damage from voidwaker by 1.5 and compare this against the extra damage from zcb.
A more generalised approach is to divide the extra damage by the spec cost for every spec weapon to get the extra damage per spec energy:

Extra Damage per Spec Energy = (Spec DPS − Main DPS) × Attack Speed ÷ Spec Cost

This gives a normalized number to compare the effectiveness of different special weapons. The higher the extra damage per spec energy, the more efficient the weapon is for special attacks.
